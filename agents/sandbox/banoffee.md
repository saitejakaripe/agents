# Banoffee Pie Recipe

## Description
This shockingly easy banoffee pie combines a crunchy graham cracker crust, soft and sweet dulce de leche, thick slices of fresh banana, and a mountain of billowy whipped cream. Everyone who tries a slice is instantly hooked.

## Ingredients

### Crust
- 1 1/2 cups (180g) graham cracker crumbs (about 12 full sheet graham crackers)
- 1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar
- 6 tablespoons (85g) unsalted butter, melted

### Filling
- 1 1/4 cups dulce de leche (see notes on making it from sweetened condensed milk)
- 2 large bananas, sliced

### Whipped Cream
- 2 cups (480ml) cold heavy cream or heavy whipping cream
- 1/4 cup confectioners sugar or granulated sugar
- 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

### Optional Garnish
- Chocolate shavings, chopped nuts, or toffee bits

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 3506F (1776C).
2. Make the crust: Mix graham cracker crumbs and sugar. Stir in melted butter until thick and sandy. Press into an ungreased 9-inch pie dish, up sides with medium pressure. Bake 15 minutes and then cool.
3. For the whipped cream: Whip the heavy cream, sugar, and vanilla on medium-high speed until medium peaks form (3-4 minutes).
4. Spread dulce de leche on the cooled crust.
5. Arrange banana slices in 12 layers over dulce de leche.
6. Spread whipped cream over bananas.
7. Refrigerate uncovered for at least 2 hours or up to 1 day.
8. Garnish with desired toppings before serving.
9. Store leftovers covered in refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Notes

- **Dulce de Leche Made from Sweetened Condensed Milk**: Pour sweetened condensed milk in a pie dish. Place pie dish in a large roasting pan filled halfway with hot water. Cover loosely with foil. Bake at 3506F for about 2 hours until thickened and caramelized.
- **Bananas Browning**: Optional lemon juice on bananas can prevent browning.
- **Substitutions**: Digestive biscuits can substitute graham crackers.
- **Make Ahead**: Crust can be refrigerated or frozen before assembly. Dulce de leche can be made up to 2 weeks in advance.

## Yield
8-10 servings

## Total Time
3 hours 35 minutes

Enjoy your delicious Banoffee Pie!
